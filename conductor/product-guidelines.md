# Product Guidelines: ClinicCare

## 🎨 Branding & Visual Identity
- **Logo:** Use the primary logo (`ClinicCare-logo.png`) for all top-level branding.
- **Color Palette:**
  - **Primary:** Deep Blue (Trust, Clinical) - `#1e40af` (Blue 800)
  - **Secondary:** Clinical Teal (Health, Care) - `#0d9488` (Teal 600)
  - **Accent:** Orange (Alerts, Highlights) - `#f97316` (Orange 500)
- **Typography:**
  - **Headings:** Sans-serif, bold for authority and clarity (e.g., Inter or Geist).
  - **Body:** Readable sans-serif with generous line height for clinical data reading.
- **Iconography:** Use a consistent set of Lucide icons to represent modules and actions.

## 💎 UX Principles
- **Clarity over Cleverness:** Ensure that clinical data is never obscured by complex animations or layouts.
- **Single Source of Truth:** Every view should represent the most current state of the database.
- **Accessibility (a11y):** Maintain WCAG 2.1 compliance, ensuring high contrast and screen reader compatibility.
- **Responsive Design:** Interfaces must be fully functional on desktops (admin work) and optimized for tablets (bedside care).
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Hide advanced clinical fields until they are needed to prevent user overwhelm.

## ✍️ Prose & Communication Style
- **Tone:** Professional, empathetic, and authoritative.
- **Terminology:** Strictly adhere to medical and institutional standards (e.g., use "Resident" instead of "Patient" where appropriate for ILPIs).
- **Feedback:** Provide clear, non-alarming success/error messages for all data operations.
- **Language:** Primary interface in Portuguese (PT-BR) to align with local institutional needs.
